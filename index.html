Mediocre Inventions


This website is for documenting my experiments with different combinations of technology stacks. These will usually be in the form of tutorials rather than blog posts about the experience. Some topics are more general and already covered in other sites. However, most of the articles moving forward will be tutorials for using less conventional technology.

I try not to use cutting edge technology. Instead, I prefer to use technology that has been around for a while. This is because they tend to require more effort to integrate with newer concepts.
